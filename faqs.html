Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our handcrafted Kashmiri shawls, orders, delivery, and care.

What materials are used in your shawls?

Our shawls are made using premium materials such as pure Pashmina, wool, and fine blends, sourced and crafted traditionally by Kashmiri artisans.

Do you ship all over India?

Yes, we deliver across India. Shipping timelines may vary depending on your city or region.

Do you offer international shipping?

International shipping is available on request. Please contact us via WhatsApp to check availability and shipping charges.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by clicking the Buy or WhatsApp button on the product page and chatting with our team directly.

Are the product images real?

Yes. All product images are of actual shawls. Minor color variations may occur due to lighting or screen differences.

Can I return or exchange a shawl?

Returns or exchanges are accepted only in case of defects or damage. Please contact us within 24 hours of delivery.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?

Yes, we accept bulk and wholesale orders for boutiques and events. Contact us directly to discuss pricing and customization.

How do I know my shawl is authentic?

Each shawl reflects traditional craftsmanship, hand-finishing, and authentic weaving techniques unique to Kashmir.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or through our Contact page. We usually respond within a few hours.
